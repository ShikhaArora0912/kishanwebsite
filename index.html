Co-curriculars at the college

Our co-curricular programme provides opportunities for students to experience and develop skills and personal qualities through their active participation in activities beyond the classroom.

Being active, being involved, and being challenged will always be part of being a “kiwi kid”.

Alongside Sports, Arts, Cultural Groups and Enviroschools we also provide many Service and Leadership opportunities such as Coaching, Leadership Programmes, Librarians, Umpiring and the Young New Zealanders Challenge.

This year all students will be encouraged to have a minimum of four co-curricular activities listed on each of their reports. We believe that all students should be actively participating in co-curricular activities and it is great to see parents, our community, and staff getting involved as well.
