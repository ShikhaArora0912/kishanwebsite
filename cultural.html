Cultural

Allowing students to come together and display their own culture in front of their peers and the whole college. No matter where we come from, or what our culture is; we are all New Zealanders and students of Botany Downs Secondary College.

CULTURAL GROUPS

Cultural groups are open to any of our students. Once enrolled, a high level of commitment is expected as of any member of a sports teams. Participating students could be representing the college in various cultural events during the year.

Many groups rely on outside tutors to coach our students and have a cost attached to the participation. Information on Cultural Co-curricular activities on offer this year and how you can get involved is below.

Chinese Dance

Embrace Chinese culture through dance! Join us to learn intricate moves, connect with tradition, and captivate with graceful performances. Let's dance the essence of China!

Chinese Lion Dance

Experience the thrill of Chinese Lion Dance! Join us to embody tradition, showcase vibrant performances, and roar with cultural pride. Let's dance as lions!

K-pop

Groove to K-pop's beat! Join us to master iconic moves, feel the music, and embrace K-pop's infectious energy. Let's dance to the K-pop tunes, BTS, BlackPink, and more!

Indian Bollywood

Experience the magic of Indian Dance! Join us to learn intricate moves, connect with cultural traditions, and captivate with mesmerizing performances. Let's dance to India's rhythms!

Samoan Dance
