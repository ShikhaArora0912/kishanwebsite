Sports

We encourage students to participate in a variety of sports whilst at college.

As a sports department, we endeavour to offer a large number of sporting codes to a wide range of students. If a particular sport is not offered, we will attempt to provide an opportunity for that student to represent BDSC in that sport.

Sports are offered all year round with Summer Sport being played in Terms 1 and 4, and Winter Sport in Terms 2 and 3.

SPORTING EVENTS

Every year Botany Downs Secondary College offers college-wide events in which every student is expected to compete. These events are Athletics and Cross Country. Both events are held in the first half of the year. Students who excel in either of these events have the opportunity to represent the college at regional and national events.

Sports Tournament Week is held during Term 3 where our top Netball, Hockey, and Football teams compete.


Our annual Sports Awards dinner is held later in the year. This is a great opportunity for the Sports Department to recognise our top teams, coaches, managers and individual students who have represented the college. ...

Our big sporting codes also have individual prize-giving awards for all teams.
Picnic Day is a fun event held for Years 9 and 10 students in the last week of the school year.

HIGH-PERFORMANCE PROGRAMME

The BDSC High Performance Programme is designed to assist students aspiring to be world class athletes both on and off the field, in their chosen sport.
The programme takes a holistic approach where athletes will be given an individualised strength and conditioning programme, they will learn about mental skills, nutrition and get support with understanding sport, school and life balance. The programme also caters for all age and stage groups with sessions for sports scholarship students, year 9 & 10 students and female only sessions.
Athletes are chosen based on their current level of play ie. New Zealand/Auckland Rep.

Netball

This is one of the largest sporting codes in our school. Netball is available for all years and abilities, from those wanting to play high level in the Premier team or those just starting out.

Squash

Botany Downs Secondary College has a number of squash teams that competitively compete against other Auckland teams. With consistent top finishes, we are a college to beat.

Rugby

Rugby at Botany Downs Secondary College has flourished since fledgling steps in 2004. After ten years the rugby programme boasts 8 teams and as many titles as any school in Auckland.

Hockey

Weekly training and games. U15 boys: Games Monday. U15 girls: Games Tuesday. Senior boys: Games Wednesday. Senior girls: Games Thursday.

Football

With eight football teams in the college, it is easy to see that football is one of Botany's popular sports.

Tennis

Tennis is offered as both a competitive and social sport at Botany Downs Secondary College. The competitive season runs through Term 1 and 4.

Touch Rugby

Touch has always been popular at Botany Downs Secondary College. Term 1 offers Senior Girls, Boys and Mix and Term 4 Junior Girls and Boys.

Basketball

We love basketball here at the college. At the start of the year there are sometimes 40-50 players vying for 10 spots on a team.

Cricket

We have a number of teams available which includes 1st XI, 2nd XI, 3rd XI, Junior Boys (Year 9 only) and 1st XI Girls.

Ultimate Frisbee

Weekly training. One day tournaments in term 1 & 4.

Volleyball

Weekly training. Weekly games on Thursday. Seniors: term 1 Juniors: term 4

Badminton

Social: Open to all. A/B grade: Trial for experienced players only.
